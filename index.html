We are AI & Data Science club of General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University.

VISION

The vision of an AI and Data Science Club would be to foster a community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about advancing the field of AI and data science, and who are committed to using their skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in their respective fields.

MISSION

The mission of an AI and Data Science Club would be to create a community of individuals who are passionate about AI and data science, and who are committed to advancing these fields in a responsible and ethical manner.

Data Odyssey 2023: A Spectacular Triumph in AI & Data Science Exploration

Step into the world of groundbreaking innovation and witness the future of data science communication at Data Odyssey 2023‚Äîan extraordinary exhibition that illuminated the potential of data in shaping a brighter tomorrow. Hosted by the DatAInspire, the AI and Data Science Club of General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University, this pioneering event introduced the revolutionary BSc. Applied Data Science Communication program, the first of its kind in Sri Lanka.

The exhibition, held in collaboration with the 16th International Research Conference at KDU, unfolded with interactive displays exploring cutting-edge algorithms, machine learning models, and visualizations that unravel complex patterns hidden within data. From predictive analytics to natural language processing, attendees engaged with the transformative power of data science across various industries.

Coordinated by Ms. Chamodhi Wickramasinghe and supported by Dr. Charith Silva and Dr. Bimali Indrarathne, the exhibition featured a distinguished panel of judges, including industry experts and academic leaders. Teams showcased their AI and Data Science projects, with Team BIN TECH EXPLORE securing the first place for the Smart Intelligent Segregator, Team LUMOS clinching second place for the Smart Street Light System, and Team TROJANS securing the third place for the Sensory Safe Cap.

Rear Admiral HGU Dammika Kumara, Vice Chancellor of KDU, graced the event as the chief guest, further emphasizing the significance of this momentous occasion. From data exploration to actionable insights, Data Odyssey 2023 showcased the transformative power of data and its potential to shape a brighter future.

DATA SCIENCE CLUB OF GENERAL SIR JOHN KOTELAWALA DEFENCE UNIVERSITY HOLDS FIRST INSTALLATION CEREMONY

Data Science Club of the General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University held its first installation ceremony at on 25th April 2023. Chief guest of the event was Deputy Vice Chancellor, defence and administration of KDU, Brigadier DCA Wickramasinghe, USP,USACGSA. The event attended by students, faculty members, and industry representatives.

This club was initiated by the undergraduates who follows BSc. Applied Data science communication degree program of languages department, Faculty of management social sciences and humanities. Head of the department, major Wimansa Abeywickrama and the course coordinator Ms. Esala Malawithanthri are the pillars of the success of the event as they encouraged the undergraduates to reach the spirited heights in industry.

The ceremony began with a welcome address by Mishka Marzook, the president of the club president, spoke about the club's mission to create a community of individuals who are passionate about AI and data science and are committed to advancing in these fields in a responsible and ethical manner.The guest of honour of the ceremony was Dr. Charith Silva , a data scientist and an adjunct lecturer of KDU. He spoke about career paths and the challenges and rewards of working in data science and also offered advice to students who are interested in pursuing a career in this field.
